Conference Overview

The 2025 Sixth International Conference on Advances in Electronics, Computers and Communications (ICAECC) will be held at REVA University, Bangalore, India. The conference presents an open forum for scientists, researchers, and engineers to exchange the latest innovations and research advancements in the areas of next-generation electronics, computers, communication architectures, protocols and algorithms, content systems, applications, and services. The conference will include plenary sessions and invited talks from eminent researchers on the state of the art in related areas. Contributions describing original research, surveys, and applications are invited.

About IEEE Student Branch REVA University

The International Conference on Advances in Electronics, Computers and Communications (ICAECC) is one of the flagship conferences organized by IEEE Student Branch REVA University, in association with School Of Electronics & Communication Engineering, School Of Computer Science Engineering & School Of Computing & Information Technology REVA University, Bengaluru, India. The ICAECC 2021 is the fourth version which is technically sponsored by IEEE Bangalore Section. All the previous ICAECC versions – First (2014), Second (2018), Third (2020) and Fourth (2022) papers are published in IEEE Xplore Digital Library and also indexed in Scopus.

About REVA University

REVA University is a premier institution located in Bangalore, India, dedicated to providing quality education in various fields of study. The university is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life that encourages students to excel academically and personally. With a focus on research and innovation, REVA University aims to foster a culture of learning and development among its students.
